About the Initiative

As the childhood obesity epidemic continues to rise in the United States and elsewhere, the CELESTIAL CUISINE initiative is combatting the problem head-on. Working with top nutritionists, pediatricians and chefs, we’ve compiled (and continue to compile) an extensive repository of health-conscious recipes that your kid will love! With a sci-fi themed interactive website, it encourages your little one to help in and learn about the cooking process, as well.

So, what do you say? Let’s make kids healthy again!
